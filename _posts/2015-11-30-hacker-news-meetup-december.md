This time around, the Thursday night networking event will be hosted at [Daga BrewPub](http://www.smartshanghai.com/venue/12843/daga_brewpub_fuxing_lu) on Fuxing Lu. 

>100 Fuxing Xi Lu, near Yongfu Lu  
>复兴西路100号，近永福路
